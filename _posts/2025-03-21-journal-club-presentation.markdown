As part of the [NELI Group Journal Club](https://uol.de/en/psychology/neurophysiology/neli-journal-club) 
at the University of Oldenburg, I presented the following paper:

**Hjørtkjær et al. (2025)** — *Real-time control of a hearing instrument with EEG-based attention decoding*

This paper demonstrates a closed-loop system in which EEG signals are decoded in real time 
to identify which speaker a listener is attending to, and the output is used to directly 
control a hearing aid's beamforming direction. It is a significant step toward practical 
auditory attention decoding in everyday listening devices.
